# Code of Conduct

GalAid is for helping people solve visual novel launch problems without shame or gatekeeping. Many users are new to file systems, locale issues, archives, and old Windows runtime errors. Keep the project welcoming to beginners.

## Expected Behavior

- Be patient and clear when helping users diagnose issues.
- Assume people are stuck, not careless.
- Keep examples metadata-only and privacy-safe.
- Respect creators, translators, players, and archivists.
- Explain tradeoffs without mocking someone's setup or skill level.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, sexualized comments, or discriminatory language.
- Pressuring people to share private paths, logs, personal details, or game files.
- Sharing cracks, serials, DRM bypasses, decryption instructions, or unauthorized downloads.
- Posting copyrighted scripts, CG, audio, video, or extracted assets from third-party games.
- Repeatedly derailing issues away from safe local diagnosis.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to being blocked from the project.

If you need to report conduct concerns, contact a maintainer through the repository's available private channels when possible. If only public issues are available, keep the report brief and avoid private details.
